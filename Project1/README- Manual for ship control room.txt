------------------------------------------------------------------------
--------------Manual for operating in the control room------------------
------------------------------------------------------------------------

-----------------
camera movement
----------------
forward--page up
backward--page down
right--right key
left--left key
up--up key
down--down key

----------------------
rotate the scene
----------------------
< and > keys


-----------------------
navigate using wheel
-----------------------
'o' and 'p'

------------------------------------
to see officer jim operate,first move camera to a viewable position,then press
-------------------------------------
e--> t--> 5--> 7--> 9  
(press r,y,6,8,0 to stop timer functions and carry on with the rest)


---------------------------------
three meters on the back panel
----------------------------------
left---rudder(changes when navigation wheel is turned,shows the ship's yaw)
middle--speedometer
right--wind anemometer

-------------------------
turn the levers
-------------------------
left lever  q , w  
right lever u , i


------------------------
lights
------------------------
light0   on--1  off--shift+1
light1   on--2  off--shift+2
light2   is the sun,always on
